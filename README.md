# UDEMY-EDUTECH-PROJECT-ANALYSIS

![](https://github.com/0xtonyandrew1/EDUTECH-PROJECT-ANALYSIS/blob/main/172834_default-meta-image-v2%20UDEMY.avif)

## INTRODUCTION:

This ia an analysis on udemy dataset from, the year 2011 to 2017 evaluated in other to gain insight of performance on subjects with various key performanace indicators and provide recommendations that will foster academic and company's interest.


## OBJECTIVES:

1. What is the total number of paid and Unpaid course
2. What is the total number of subscribers by each subject
3. What Is the total number of subscribers by content duration
4. What is the total number of lectures and reviews by each subject
5. Create a time series of total subscribers


## Data source:

Data was sourced from Kaggle ranging from 2011-2017 and it contains 11 columns and 3672 rows.


## Data preparation:

### Cleaning: 
The cleaning process was done in power query in which we checked for blanks, duplicate, spellings and inconsistencies. The data returned no blanks, duplicates, wrong spellings or Inconsistencies, each column were checked for right data type. It proves data quality and integrity.

### Transformation: 
The naming convention was changed from a snake naming convention to a standard naming convention. The columns were re-ordered into the right format.


## Methodology:

1. A calculated measure was created to count the total number of paid and unpaid courses
2. A calculated columns was created to categorize the content duration into four categories (very short time, short time, long time, very long time).
3. Charts were used to visualize the findings of our objectives.


## Visualization:

![](https://github.com/0xtonyandrew1/EDUTECH-PROJECT-ANALYSIS/blob/main/IMG_3301.jpg)


## Findings:

1. 91.56% of subjects are paid while 8.44% were unpaid
2. Web development has the total number of subscribers
3. Very short time content duration has the most subscribers
4. Web development has the most reviews and lectures
5. The trend line shows increase in subscribers from 2011 to 2015 then a drop following 2015 to 2017


## Recommendations:

1. Creating a financial aid to discount subjects in other to increase number of subscribers
2. Encourage subscribers to take on more courses, explain why one compliments the other
3. There should be reduction in the content duration time because subscribers respond more to very short time content duration
4. There should be an educative campaign or advertisement educating the public of why taking up an online course works with schedule, increase productivity and aids career development


## Conclusion: 

The analysis deconstrates that a financial-aid can effectively boost subscribers numbers by making courses more affordable, while simultaneosly encouraging leaners to enroll in additional subjects. shorter content duration drive higher engagement, complementing these tactics with an educative advertising campaign which highlight how online study fits into busy schedule.
